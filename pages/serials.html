Learning English from TV shows is almost identical to movies, but TV shows obviously last longer, that is, you can immerse yourself in the plot and forget that you are watching in a foreign language.

At the moment, TV shows are not only entertainment, but also a reflection of social problems, traditions, thoughts of society, so, as I said before, you will learn the culture of another country.

It is recommended to watch those series that you have already viewed in Russian. Thus, you will understand exactly what is being discussed, but not in your native language.

You will begin to learn real everyday English, without pauses between each phrase, as in textbooks. You will also encounter different accents, most likely either American or British, and I am sure that you will be able to feel the difference.

Most of the jokes in English TV series consist of word games, so watching with dubbing, you may not understand them. Again, if you discuss what you have viewed in English with a person whose language level is higher than yours, you will begin to solve one of the most common problems – the language barrier, which prevents many students from developing speaking skills.

Many people watch TV shows with subtitles, but there are tips for using them. In the beginning, it is better to use double subtitles, which are available on different sites. You will immediately see both Russian and English subtitles.

Next, you can switch only to English, and in the end without them at all.

You cannot be lazy even with such a study. If you do not understand a certain moment or a dialogue - revise it, catch the subtlety in pronunciation, remember a new word or a whole stable expression.
